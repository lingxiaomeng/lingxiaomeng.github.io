Research Topics

- Evolutionary Multi-modal multi-objective optimization: Test problem design, search behavior analysis of existing algorithms, and new algorithm design.

- Indicator-based evolutionary multi-objective optimization: New algorithm design.

- Theoretical analysis of performance indicators for multi-objective optimization: Analysis of the optimal distribution of solutions for each indicator.

- Evolutionary many-objective optimization: Test problem design, search behavior analysis of existing algorithms, and new algorithm design.

- Normalization in evolutionary multi-objective optimization: Analysis of the effects of normalization mechanisms on the performance of evolutionary multi-objective optimization algorithms, and design of new normalization mechanisms.

- Interactive evolutionary multi-objective optimization.

- Applications of evolutionary multi-objective optimization such as multi-objective navigation of UAV.
